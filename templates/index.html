About Us

Transforming Aspirants into

LearnNova is an AI-powered education platform built to deliver affordable, intelligent, and skill-driven learning experiences for students of all age groups.

Vision

To make AI-based education affordable and accessible for every learner, regardless of age or background.

Mission

To support students at every stage — from school fundamentals to advanced computer skills and government exam preparation — using structured content and smart AI technology.
